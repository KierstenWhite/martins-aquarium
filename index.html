Fish Tips from Martin

When it comes to harvesting fish, there are a few precautions you should take. They include:

  • Washing the fish
  • Singing to the fish
  • Remembering the most basic motto: "Fish are friends, not food."

Best Places to Harvest Fish

These are my favorite places to harvest fish that give you a unique and welcoming experience. They include:

  • Narnia
  • Stardew Valley (particularly the mines)
  • Ginger Island
  • Neverland
  • The Wal-Mart parking lot
  • Santa's Workshop

Keeping the Fishies Safe

There are a lot of ways we need to keep the fishies safe. They should be salted and heated to 180 degrees in the oven. Crispy skin is the best to protect them from sharks. When scubaing to harvest the fish, don't worry about using a tank. It will wear you down. Lastly, dangerous fish are actually just trying to say hello. So, pet them if you can.
